iQube

iQube is a handheld, cube-shaped gaming platform optmized for puzzle games. iQube teaches problem solving skills, enriches math skills and fosters mental agility. With it's engaging tactile interactions iQube is a platform for new and diverse game types. It's the Rubik's Cube of the 21st century.

The Device

iQube features 6 8x8 LED matrix displays, a 6-axis Accelerometer/gyroscope motion chip, and a piezoelectric buzzer for fun 8-bit sounds all of which is mounted snuggly in a custom 3D-printed enclosure.

Measuring only 2.2in in each dimension and weighing in at around 4oz, iQube fits nicely in hands of all sizes and feels sturdy yet playful.

Games

iQube is the perfect platform for games invoving Sequences and Patterns, Codes and Ciphers, Memory Games and Sudoku-esque Games.
