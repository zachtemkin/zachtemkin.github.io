BioBrick

Bio Brick is a modular, hydroponic roof gardening system I worked to develop with Kris Holmquist, Lauren Menard, Luke Buscher, Xiaohan Li and Brittany Thomas for PDI Studio II at RPI. This is a brief statement about Bio Brick:

Urban areas support such a large portion of our planet's population but urban life can be stressful. Loud noises, dirty air, tight spaces. We at Bio Brick wanted to make urban living healthier, happier and greener. When studying urban environments one thing that jumped out at us was that the tops of buildings were a huge source of space that was going to waster so we decided to use them for growing food and so Bio Brick was born.
